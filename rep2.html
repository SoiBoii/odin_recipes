Steak

Ingredients

  • Prefered cut of beef
  • Butter 30g
  • Rosemary and Thyme
  • Garlic cloves

Method

  1. Sear the steak

    Heat a cast iron skillet and sear the steak using the rendered out fat of the cut of beef.

  2. Baste the steak:

    Add butter to the pan and let it melt. Once the butter is melted add the herbs and garlic cloves to the butter and baste the steak with it to infuse the flavours of the herbs and garlic and also cook the steak.

  3. Cut and serve the steak:

    Cut the steak into bite sized pieces and enjoy!!
