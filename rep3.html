Milkshake

Ingredients

  • Full fat milk
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Ice cubes
  • Chocolate Syrup

Method

  1. Blend the ingredients:

    Add milk, ice cream and ice cubes to a blender and blend until all the ingredients are blended.

  2. Prep the glass and serve:

    Drizzle some chocolate syrup on the inside of a large glass and pour the milkshake into it. The milkshake is now ready to serve.
